About

"There is too much Energy conversation instead of Energy conservation! Let us walk the talk!"

This in short, summaries the attitude of BMSCE's IEEE Power and Energy society. This nascent community is one of the very few communities in BMSCE associated with sustainable energy, it's various applications and so on! It is a group of very versatile and talented individuals, whose one vision is to provide all means to support innovation in the field of Power and Energy.

Events

Smart Grid

25th April, 2019

This was a technical talk presented by Mr. Arun Kumar from Electrono Solutions. He spoke about how much power wasted is per day which gave the audience an insight on how vital it is to reduce power losses, wastage and work towards energy efficiency. There was also a video on the working and efficiency of Smart Grids.

Codigo

25th April, 2019

The event was a classic code-a-thon made in collaboration with ISE Student Club. The main theme of the event was energy optimization. The participants had to solve 6 questions, made by ISE Student Club according to the specifications of our society, with a time limit of 2 hours on HackerRank.

In-quiz-ition

25th April, 2019

This was a quiz made in collaboration with the Quiz Club of BMSCE, QCaine. The initial round consisted of 20 general knowledge questions and the top 6 teams were considered for the finals. At the end of the finals, the top two teams were selected as the winners. The event received a good feedback.

Science of Cryptography

25th April, 2019

This workshop was conducted in collaboration with the Anonymous Club, BMSCE. It began with an introduction to cryptography, its types and then proceeded to Symmetric & Asymmetric cryptographic techniques and the many modes of encryption. It concluded with a hands-on session where the participants learned to use encryptions.
